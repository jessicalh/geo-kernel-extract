2//
3// CoulombResult: vacuum Coulomb E-field and EFG at each atom.
4//
5// For each atom, computes the electric field E and electric field gradient
6// tensor V from all other partial charges. Decomposes by source:
7// backbone, sidechain, aromatic. Computes solvent contribution as
8// APBS - vacuum total (if ApbsFieldResult is present).
9//
10// The EFG tensor V_ab = sum_j q_j * K_ab(r_ij) uses the same dipolar
11// kernel as McConnell:
12// K_ab = (3 d_a d_b / r^5 - delta_ab / r^3)
13//
14// Each term is traceless by Gauss's law (no charge at the field point).
15// The sum is traceless. Traceless projection applied after accumulation
16// to correct floating-point drift.
17//
18// Units:
19// E-field: V/A (raw sum in e/A^2, multiplied by COULOMB_KE = 14.3996)
20// EFG: V/A^2 (raw sum in e/A^3, multiplied by COULOMB_KE)
21// Same units as ApbsFieldResult for direct comparison.
22//
