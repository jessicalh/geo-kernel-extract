2//
3// PropkaProtonator: pKa prediction via PROPKA 3.5.
4//
5// Calls the propka3 binary on a temp PDB (heavy atoms only — PROPKA
6// works on heavy atoms), parses the .pka output for per-residue pKa
7// values, and applies Henderson-Hasselbalch at the target pH to produce
8// a ProtonationState.
9//
10// The naming boundary is in Protonate(): PROPKA output uses PDB residue
11// names and numbers. We match to our typed Protein residues by sequence
12// number and amino acid type. After matching, everything is typed.
13//
14// PROPKA citation: Olsson et al. (2011) JCTC 7:525.
15//
