2//
3// McConnellResult: Bond magnetic anisotropy shielding tensors.
4//
5// For each bond within 10A of each atom, computes the full McConnell
6// shielding tensor (asymmetric, non-traceless, T0+T1+T2) and the
7// symmetric traceless dipolar kernel K. Accumulates per-category
8// totals and tracks nearest CO/CN bonds.
9//
10// The full tensor formula (from GEOMETRIC_KERNEL_CATALOGUE.md):
11//
12// M_ab = 9 cos_theta d_hat_a b_hat_b
13// - 3 b_hat_a b_hat_b
14// - (3 d_hat_a d_hat_b - delta_ab)
15//
16// Shielding contribution = M_ab / r^3 (Angstrom^-3)
17//
18// The dipolar kernel K_ab = (3 d_hat_a d_hat_b - delta_ab) / r^3
19// is the symmetric traceless part, stored separately for features.
20//
21// The McConnell scalar f = (3 cos^2 theta - 1) / r^3 is the double
22// contraction of K with the bond direction. It is NOT the tensor trace
23// (which comes from the full M, not from K alone).
24//
