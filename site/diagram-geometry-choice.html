4. GeometryChoice Flow

Every calculator holds a KernelFilterSet. Before evaluating a kernel, five filters must accept. During Compute(), calculators record geometric decisions via GeometryChoiceBuilder: entities, roles, outcomes, named numbers with units, rejection reasons, and optional field samplers for UI visualisation.
